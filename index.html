About Us

Welcome to BraveSpaces Creative India, your one-stop resource for fostering consent-based practices and creating authentic narratives around intimacy, sex, sexuality, and gender in the Indian entertainment industry. We are a team of Intimacy Professionals who pioneer in fostering consent-forward practices and elevating narratives around intimacy, sexuality, and gender in creative spaces. We are here to empower creators and performers as they tell powerful stories with respect and authenticity. We champion the de-tabooing of intimacy, nudity, sex, sexuality and gender spectrum through film and stage. Our approach prioritizes consent, courage, and sensitivity, while remaining deeply committed to representation, aesthetics, and realism in artistic narratives. We believe in empowering individuals with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to navigate complex narratives, fostering a community where consent and agency are paramount.

Our Mission

To cultivate braver, secure and more accessible environments across the screen and stage, in the realms of intimacy direction, consent education, and gender and sexuality representation, therefore empowering artists and individuals to ensure that every creative endeavor is respectful, informed, and groundbreaking.

Our Vision

To create a world where creative expressions are free of stereotypes and constraints, enabling truthful and sensitive explorations of intimacy; breaking barriers and taboos to set new standards for portrayals of intimacy, sexuality, and gender representation in film, television, theater, and digital content.

Workshops

Our workshops are designed with a deep understanding of India’s diverse cultural landscape. This means not only navigating the complexities of consent and agency but doing so in a way that honors the traditions, values, and sensibilities inherent to Indian storytelling.

We offer a range of workshops designed to empower individuals and unlock artistic potential:

On Set Trainings

We provide expert consulting services for on-set processes, ensuring narratives involving themes of intimacy, sexuality, and gender are approached with the utmost care, respect, and authenticity. Each training is designed specifically for the project keeping in mind the storytelling nuances.

Acting Intimacy (7 days)

A transformative 7-day module that empowers actors to connect with their bodies, assert their agency, and clearly define their boundaries and articulate consent. This module is meant for actors to find confidence in expressing themselves in scenes and contexts that require high vulnerability both physically and emotionally. This workshop culminates in a scene work presentation, showcasing the actors' journey towards owning their space.

Own Your Body (1 day)

In this one-day module, participants engage with their vagus nerve through breath and movement, accessing a deeper connection with their body’s agency. It’s an invitation to everyone, actor or not, to explore and understand the power of their physical presence. This introductory workshop provides tools for anyone to connect with their body and to develop a deeper understanding of their body’s vocabulary, boundaries, and personal agency.

Intimacy for Directors

This workshop equips directors with the skills to articulate intimate scenes from visual, performance, and gaze perspectives. It fosters an understanding of Intimacy Direction and collaboration with actors.

Movement Workshops

Our diverse lineup includes Stick work, Kalaripayattu, Capoeira, and workshops on contact improvisation and fluid movement, empowering actors with a range of physical storytelling tools.

Educational Institute Trainings

We design bespoke training modules for educational institutions, focusing on performance, communication, intimacy, and consent-based practices. These sessions are crafted to meet the specific needs of each institute, ensuring relevance and impact.
